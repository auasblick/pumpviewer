UJAMS Pumpviewer


General information:
The pumpviewer is designed to modify characteristic pump curves.
It can create new curves, and modify existing ones to find the
most efficient pump in its most efficient operating state for a
certain use case.


Overview:
 - main
	central unification of the other modules
 - generationing
	module to generate pump curves from graphic information
	sheets
 - positioning
	module which is responsible for the graphic order of the
	window
